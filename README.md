Project Description – Empower-SL

Overview:
Empower-SL is a digital platform designed to uplift underprivileged communities in Sri Lanka by connecting people who want to help with those in need. It provides a safe, efficient, and easy-to-use way for donors to contribute while offering opportunities for beneficiaries to receive support, gain employment, and develop new skills.

Challenges Addressed:
Many individuals in Sri Lanka face poverty and unemployment, yet donors often struggle to reach them effectively. Additionally, people who want to improve their situation through learning or work-related opportunities may not have access to resources. There is a gap between those who can give and those who need assistance, as well as a lack of centralized online platforms to facilitate these connections.

Solution Provided:
Empower-SL acts as a bridge between donors and beneficiaries. The platform allows donors to make contributions online and beneficiaries to submit requests for support. Beyond financial assistance, users can explore job opportunities, post small business ideas, and access free courses to enhance their skills. This integrated approach ensures that people not only receive aid but are empowered to become self-sufficient over time.

Key User Groups:

Donors: Individuals or organizations providing support.,
Beneficiaries: People in need of financial help, jobs, or skill development opportunities.,
Administrators: Manage the system, oversee donations, job posts, and course enrollments.
